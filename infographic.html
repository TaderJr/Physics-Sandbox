About Physics Sandbox

Physics Sandbox v1.5.0 is an advanced particle simulation game featuring realistic physics, temperature systems, and complex element interactions. Create, experiment, and watch as your creations come to life through sophisticated particle dynamics.

Key Features

  • 27 Unique Elements: From basic materials like sand and water to specialized substances like black holes and liquid nitrogen
  • Advanced Water Physics: Realistic liquid flow with proper density-based interactions
  • Temperature System: Heat transfer, phase changes, and thermal vision mode
  • Plant Life System: Seeds grow into plants with unlimited vertical growth
  • Chemical Reactions: Fire spreads, explosions occur, and materials interact realistically
  • Customizable Simulation: Adjustable speed, brush size, and grid resolution
  • Gravitational Effects: Black holes create dramatic particle absorption
  • Cryogenic Physics: Liquid nitrogen creates brittle ice with realistic fracturing

Game Mechanics

Water Physics System

The water physics engine has been completely overhauled in version 1.4.2. Key improvements include:

  • Density-Based Displacement: Heavier particles now sink through water naturally instead of stacking on top
  • Realistic Flow Mechanics: Water flows and settles more naturally, following proper fluid dynamics
  • Improved Settling: Liquids find their equilibrium state more efficiently
  • Multi-Layer Interactions: Different density liquids properly separate and layer

Temperature System

Advanced thermal simulation affects all particles in the sandbox:

  • Heat Transfer: Temperature spreads between adjacent particles over time
  • Universal Cooling: All heated particles gradually return to room temperature (20°C)
  • Phase Transitions: Elements change state based on temperature thresholds
  • Thermal Vision: Special viewing mode shows temperature as colors
  • Selective Ice Melting: Ice only melts when in contact with hot materials
Temperature Color Scale:
Below -50°C (Deep Blue)
-50°C to 0°C (Blue)
0°C to 50°C (Light Blue)
50°C to 100°C (Green)
100°C to 200°C (Yellow)
200°C to 400°C (Orange)
400°C to 600°C (Red-Orange)
Above 600°C (Red)

Plant Growth System

Enhanced biological simulation with unlimited growth potential:

  • Simplified Requirements: Seeds now only need soil contact and suitable temperature (5°C-45°C)
  • Unlimited Growth: Plants can grow infinitely high with no height restrictions
  • Leaf Generation: Mature plants spread leaves horizontally around growth segments
  • Flower Production: Plants 4+ segments tall can generate colorful flowers
  • Growth Stages: Seed → Sprout (65 ticks) → Plant (100 ticks) → Mature Plant (150 ticks/segment)

Special Mechanics

Gravitational Effects

Black holes create powerful suction that pulls particles within a 10-unit radius into their center, consuming all matter.

Cryogenic Physics

Liquid nitrogen instantly freezes materials into brittle ice that shatters when heated or impacted.

Viral Spread

Virus particles consume and convert any material type they contact, creating rapid infection chains.

Volcanic Activity

Volcano cores continuously generate lava, creating dynamic geological features and natural disasters.

Toggle between normal element colors and temperature-based color visualization.

Interface Layout

  • Resizable Controls: Control panel can be resized by dragging the handle at the top
  • Responsive Design: Interface adapts to different screen sizes automatically
  • Touch Support: Full mobile and tablet compatibility
  • What's New Button: Quick access to latest features and updates
  • Improved Spacing: Better visual hierarchy with reduced neon effects

Performance Tips

  • Larger Grid Size: Use 4px+ for better performance with large simulations
  • Speed Control: Reduce speed if simulation becomes choppy
  • Resize Controls: Adjust panel size for optimal screen usage
  • Clear Regularly: Reset simulation periodically to maintain smooth performance
  • Limit Black Holes: Too many can impact performance due to gravity calculations
